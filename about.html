Our Details

We presently have five Intensive Programs: Junior IP, Elementary IP, Intermediate IP, Advanced IP and Grad IP. Auditions are open to anyone interested in entering the program. Students can audition for the following Intensive Program classes: Ballet & Jazz (two classes per week for both), Tap, Lyrical and Hip Hop.
